Self Love

Self-love is a state of appreciation for oneself that grows from actions that support our physical, psychological and spiritual growth. Self-love means having a high regard for your own well-being and happiness.Self-love can mean something different for each person because we all have many different ways to take care of ourselves. Figuring out what self-love looks like for you as an individual is an important part of your mental health.

Why should you love yourself?

You cannot pour from an empty cup.
Loving yourself provides you with self-confidence, self-worth, and in general, you feel more positive. Accepting the pain and allowing yourself to be honest with who you are is a big step to loving yourself.



What does Self Love mean?

  • Talking to and about yourself with love
  • Giving yourself a break from self-judgement
  • Trusting yourself
  • Setting healthy boundaries
  • Forgiving yourself when you aren’t being true or nice to yourself
